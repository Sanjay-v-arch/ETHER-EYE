Our Features

Wallet/Transaction Tracing

Input wallet addresses or transaction hashes to trace multi-hop transactions across the blockchain, with support for Ethereum and Solana.

Interactive Graph Visualization

Visualize transaction flows with interactive graphs, featuring zoom, pan, and click-to-expand functionality. Suspicious flows are highlighted in red.

Real-Time Streaming Dashboard

Monitor tracked wallets with live updates on new transactions, including hash, value, and timestamp, with options to pause or resume updates.

Anomaly Detection Display

Detect suspicious patterns like high-value transfers or mixer usage, with alerts displayed on the graph or in a dedicated panel.

Alerts History

View a log of all alerts sent to Discord, Telegram, or email, with an option to export as a CSV file.

Settings/Configuration

Customize tracing depth, alert thresholds, and other preferences to tailor the experience to your needs.
