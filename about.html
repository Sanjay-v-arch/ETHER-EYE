About ETHER-EYE

We’re on a mission to bring transparency and security to blockchain transactions through advanced analytics and visualization.

Our Story

ETHER-EYE was founded in 2023 by a group of students currently pursuing their undergraduate degree in Computer Science and Engineering, specializing in IoT, Cybersecurity, and Blockchain Technology. As part of their academic journey, they identified a critical gap in the blockchain ecosystem: the lack of accessible, user-friendly tools for tracking and analyzing transactions in real-time.

Driven by a passion for innovation and a commitment to enhancing blockchain security, the team developed ETHER-EYE as their final-year project. What started as a classroom idea quickly evolved into a powerful platform, thanks to their combined expertise in blockchain technology, data analytics, and user interface design. They collaborated with industry mentors and participated in hackathons, refining their tool to meet the needs of both individual users and enterprises.

Today, ETHER-EYE serves individuals, businesses, and law enforcement agencies, providing them with the insights needed to navigate the complex world of blockchain transactions safely and effectively. The team continues to innovate, with plans to integrate AI-driven anomaly detection and expand support for more blockchain networks in the future.

Our Features

Wallet/Transaction Tracing

Input wallet addresses or transaction hashes to trace multi-hop transactions across the blockchain, with support for Ethereum and Solana.

Interactive Graph Visualization

Visualize transaction flows with interactive graphs, featuring zoom, pan, and click-to-expand functionality. Suspicious flows are highlighted in red.

Real-Time Streaming Dashboard

Monitor tracked wallets with live updates on new transactions, including hash, value, and timestamp, with options to pause or resume updates.

Anomaly Detection Display

Detect suspicious patterns like high-value transfers or mixer usage, with alerts displayed on the graph or in a dedicated panel.

Alerts History

View a log of all alerts sent to Discord, Telegram, or email, with an option to export as a CSV file.

Settings/Configuration

Customize tracing depth, alert thresholds, and other preferences to tailor the experience to your needs.
